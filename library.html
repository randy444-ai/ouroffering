Ripples in the Field of Consciousness

September 7, 2025 • A contemplation

“You are so accustomed to feeling yourself as a center that this habit contracts you away from your wholeness. You go away from the vast nothing in order to be a small something. You go away from your Absence to create a presence.”

— Jean Klein

The Contradiction of Being Human

Being human is a contradictory experience. In the non-dual reality, to be human is simply one of an infinite number of expressions of Conscious Awareness.

Living in the Field

Humans live within Conscious Awareness; Conscious Awareness does not live within humans. Like all appearances, we are temporary ripples in an endless field of Conscious Awareness.

The Forgetting

The human ripple, forgetting it is only a ripple, imagines itself separate from the field. In this forgetting, it falls victim to illusion, going away from vast nothing in order to be a small something. In that illusion of separation, we sacrifice the peace and happiness that are our true nature.

The Remembering

The recognition of forgetting is itself the beginning of remembering. When the ripple remembers its essence, it sees that it never left the field. Peace is restored, not by gaining something new, but by seeing through the illusion of being separate.
