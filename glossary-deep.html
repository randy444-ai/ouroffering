Deep Glossary

Soul

Essence of our being—the quiet inner light that stands prior to personality and judgment. Not attained but remembered; it “knows the way” back to our non-dual nature.

When attention rests in Soul, life’s impressions are digested into understanding rather than recycled as struggle. See also: Food Triad, Law of the Vacuum.

Mind–Body–Spirit

Mind is Consciousness expressing (not thinking in opinions/memory). Body is formed expression within that field. Spirit is the living energy of the field (“the entire ocean in a drop”).

Right relation: Mind expresses, Body participates gratefully, Spirit energizes. Misrelation: personality tries to control. See also: Self-Remembering.

Nonduality

Not-two. The apparent multiplicity is expression within one Consciousness. Pointed to as Tao, Brahman, OM, “I and the Father are One.” Intellectual grasp helps; direct recognition liberates.

Magnetic Center

The inner attractor that draws experiences as “medicine.” With awareness, patterns are seen and can be re-educated; energy transforms into nourishment for Soul.

Four Levels of Consciousness

  1. Sleep (dreaming)
  2. Waking sleep (habituated identities act)
  3. Awakening (recognition and inquiry)
  4. Persistent aware presence (nonjudgmental Now)

Self-Remembering

Actively recognizing “I am awareness” and sensing the living presence of Consciousness now. Nourishes Soul and dissolves misperception.

Practice: ask “Am I aware?” and rest as the answer. See: STOP.

Self-Observation

Noticing identities as they arise—without judgment. Names patterns, reduces waste of energy, and opens room for forgiveness and new action.

Food Triad (Esoteric Alchemy)

Three “foods”: physical food, air, and impressions. With awareness, impressions are digested into understanding (lead → gold), feeding Soul rather than personality.

Law of the Vacuum

Emptying ourselves of habitual judgments creates space. In the clear space, Soul’s understanding flows and the flower of true Self appears.

Law of Assumption

We can “take on” the qualities of Consciousness now—nonduality, fullness, gratitude, forgiveness, peace, love—letting experience conform to clarity rather than fear.

Positive Emotion

The felt tone of higher love when suffering is allowed to pass through without resistance. Contagious in the best way; it lightens collective mind.

Trap of Time

Time belongs to the world of things. Awareness is timeless. Anxiety fades when we live the Now; Soul speaks only here.

STOP

Pause → Breathe → Remember. A one-breath return to Now that converts any moment into nourishment for Soul.
