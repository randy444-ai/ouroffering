Resources

A guide to voices and practices that echo the simple recognition: we are expressions of eternal Consciousness.

Core Voices

  • Rumi — poetry that points beyond duality to ecstatic wholeness.
  • Lao Tzu — the Tao as the ever-present, indescribable nature of One that is All.
  • Jesus — non-judgment, forgiveness, and “I and the Father are One.”
  • Siddhartha Gautama — direct seeing; emptiness as fullness.

Contemporary Guides

  • Rupert Spira — official site  ·  YouTube
  • Jean Klein — overview & archives  ·  Collected quotes & pointers
  • Eckhart Tolle — official site  ·  Eckhart Tolle Foundation  ·  YouTube
  • Fourth Way (Ouspensky) — key resources:
    • Ouspensky On Self-remembering (London)

Interesting Links

Practices You Can Try

  • Self-Remembering — noticing you are awareness.
  • Self-Observation — seeing identities without judgment.
  • STOP — pause, breathe, return to Now.
