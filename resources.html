Resources

A living guide to voices and practices that echo our simple recognition: we are expressions of eternal Consciousness.

Core Voices

  • Rumi — poetry that points beyond judgment to ecstatic wholeness.
  • Lao Tzu — the Tao as the ever-present, indescribable nature.
  • Jesus — non-judgment, forgiveness, and “I and the Father are One.”
  • Siddhartha Gautama — direct seeing; emptiness as openness.

Contemporary Guides

  • Rupert Spira — the nature of awareness, timeless presence.
  • Jean Klein — effortless being; relaxing the “small self.”
  • Eckhart Tolle — the power of Now; freedom from time-mind.
  • Fourth Way (Gurdjieff/Ouspensky) — Self-Observation & Self-Remembering.

Bridges for Curious Minds

  • Physics & Nonduality — Schrödinger’s intuition of basic non-duality.
  • Esoteric Alchemy — the Food Triad as transformation (lead → gold).

Practices You Can Try

  • Self-Remembering — noticing you are awareness.
  • Self-Observation — seeing identities without judgment.
  • STOP — pause, breathe, return to Now.

From Our Library

  • Soul — essence as quiet inner light.
  • Mind–Body–Spirit — one field, many expressions.
  • Nonduality — not-two reality.
