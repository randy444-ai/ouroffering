🌌 We Must Remember

Published September 7, 2025

“You are so accustomed to feeling yourself as a center that this habit contracts you away from your wholeness.
You go away from the vast nothing in order to be a small something.
You go away from your Absence to create a presence.”
— Jean Klein

In non-dual reality, being human is simply one of infinite experiences arising within the field of Conscious Awareness.

We only imagine ourselves to be a separate being among many separate beings. In that imagination, we contract—mistaking ourselves for a fragment rather than the whole. We turn away from our innate vastness to become a small, defined something, and in that contraction we obscure the peace and happiness that have never truly been absent.

We must remember:

🌌 We live within Conscious Awareness; Conscious Awareness does not live within us.

From this recognition, peace is not attained—it is experienced.
Happiness is not created—it is uncovered.
The vast no-thing-ness we overlook is not empty; it is overflowing with everything we truly are.
